IMPORT

An import is a good or service bought in one country that was produced in another. Imported goods or services are attractive when domestic industries cannot produce similar goods and services cheaply or efficiently. Importation and exportation are the defining financial transactions of international trade..

INDENTING

The main goal and objective of indenting business is to build a strong relationship with both customer and supplier. Got connects manufacturers to the end users for the supply of products throughout the world which include businesses that specialize in technical support, distribution, shipping, importing or exporting, marketing, etc.

EXPORT

An export in international trade is a good produced in one country that is sold into another country or a service provided in one country for the resident of another country. Exports are often referred to when speaking about international trade which is simply the exchange of goods and services with other countries..
